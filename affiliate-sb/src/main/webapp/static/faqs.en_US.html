FAQ (Frequently Asked Questions)

1. What is Affiliate Network Program?

BETMART Affiliates Network Program is a partnership program where you will earn commissions based on players that you refer to betmart.com from your Website(s) via online or offline via referrals. It's an easy way to generate income from traffic you already have on your website.

2. How do I join?

BETMART Affiliates Network Program is completely free to join. Simply fill out our Affiliate application form www.betmart.com/affiliate/web/register and you will be contacted within 72 hours (3 business days) regarding your acceptance into the program. If your application is accepted, you will receive an email containing your unique Affiliate ID, giving you access to banners, text links, and other content and marketing tools to add to your websites. Each Affiliate shall have only one account.

3. What if I don't have website. Can I still join?

Please email us at cs@betmart.com for more information.

4. How do I change my account information and what if I forgot my password?

You can change your account information by clicking on Account and then going to Update Account details on the menu. If you need further assistance, you can contact our customer service through cs@betmart.com or live chat.

5. How much can I earn?

BETMART can offer you the most competitive commission program in the market where you can earn up to 45% of commission on the referrals that you make. We provide online statistics for you 24 hours a day. You can login to your BETMART Affiliate account to check your commission status, statistics and various reports.

6. How am I paid and which payment method can I choose?

Your Affiliate commissions are personal to you and you shall have no claim to Affiliate commission or other compensation on business secured by or through persons or entities other than you. You cannot withdraw payments on behalf of another third party. Your commission will be paid to your bank account designated in your Affiliate application form in the currency of our choice. Payment will be made by wire, player account transfer, or any other method as we decide. However, we will use reasonable endeavours to accommodate your preferred payment method. You will only be paid in accordance with your sole account.

7. When will I get my commissions?

All commissions shall be paid to you on a monthly basis.

8. Do negative balances carry over?

No, negative balances will not carry over from month to month.

9. What happens with fraud and charge backs?

If an account is considered fraudulent or has charge backs, it will be disabled. Commissions are not earned on disabled accounts.

10. Does Betmart have a policy on spamming?

Yes, BETMART will not tolerate Affiliates who engage in spam activities. If an Affiliate is using spamming practices, their accounts will be closed immediately and permanently banned from our BETMART Affiliate Network Program and any payments will be forfeited.

11. Where do I get banners and content?

Once we've accepted your application, you can get any banners and content by visiting our site using your unique Affiliate ID. You can then browse the suite of creatives, or search for relevant banners based on the product, language, size, type and promotion. For specific requests, please get in touch with us or email cs@betmart.com.

12. Why should I join BETMART Affiliate Network Program?

BETMART offers an excellent commission program, there is no risk, no investment required on your part and you can start earning commission the moment your application to join the BETMART Affiliation Network Program is approved.
